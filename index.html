How It Works

From Search
to Community

Getting connected to a You Inspire Now event is simple. Five steps from finding to attending — your transformation starts now.

Scroll to explore →
01
🔍
Search Your Area

Type your city, state, or zip code into the search bar. We'll show you every upcoming event in your community — from workshops to celebrations.

02
📅
Find Your Event

Browse events by date and type. Each listing shows the full details — location, time, what to expect, and who it's designed for.

03
✅
RSVP in Seconds

Click the RSVP button, fill in your name and contact — done. You'll receive confirmation and all the details you need to show up ready.

04
🤝
Show Up & Connect

Arrive, be welcomed, and meet women on similar journeys. Our events are intentional, warm, and created to make every person feel at home.

05
✨
Leave Inspired

Walk away with tools, connections, and a renewed sense of purpose. Then invite someone — because inspiration multiplies when it's shared.
