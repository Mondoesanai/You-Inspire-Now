My Story

Hey Sista-friend,
Let me tell you about my journey.

My journey has been nothing short of a miraculous story in itself. I grew up in much turmoil — raised mostly through my teen years by a single father. I was raised to be confident, strong, and focused. But I had difficulty understanding my place as a woman, my true value, and my beauty. Although I internally struggled, no one could see it on the outside.

I finished college with two degrees, married my college sweetheart (who was fine, muscular, and headed to the NFL), and began our family. But inwardly I felt worse with time. One day — after eight years of marriage, a few children, and even living a "Christian life" — I was done. Done with religion, done with acting like I had it altogether, done with being a fruitless Christian.

That moment of surrender changed everything. My husband walked with me through a verbal dedication of my life to Christ — and my life has never been the same. I stopped the comparison, the excuses, and hiding behind the shame of my past. The very things we think will disqualify us are the very things God uses to set others free.

We're now a family of 13 — 11 children. It's been a true privilege to do life together. A transformation took place in my heart that shifted every area of my life.
