How It Works

Your Journey to Rising

Scroll to walk through the path from first contact to full transformation.

01 🌱
You Discover You Inspire Now

A friend shares it. You find us online. A flyer catches your eye. However it happens — you take the first step and reach out.

02 🤝
You Connect at a Local Event

You come to an event — maybe nervous, maybe hopeful. You meet women who are in the same season. You immediately feel like you belong.

03 🔑
You Discover Your Identity

Through community, resources, and truth — you begin to see yourself the way God sees you. The noise fades. Clarity comes.

04 🔥
You Walk in Your Purpose

Purpose isn't found — it's built. You start showing up differently: in your home, your relationships, your work. The shift is real.

05 ⬆️
You Rise — and Inspire Others

The woman who walked in needing community becomes the woman pouring into the next one. That's the You Inspire Now effect.
