Frequently Asked Questions

Use the search bar on our homepage to find groups by city, or send us a message here and we will connect you directly with a group leader in your area. We are growing rapidly — if there’s no group near you yet, ask about starting one!

All you need is a heart for women in your community and a desire to lead. We provide all the training, materials, and coaching you need. Fill out the form and select “Start a Group in My City” — our team will reach out within 48 hours.

Yes. Go Inspire Now is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. All donations are tax-deductible to the extent permitted by law. You will receive a donation receipt for every gift you make.
